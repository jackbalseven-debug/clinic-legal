Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Clinic Appointment Assistant collects, uses, and handles your information when you interact with our WhatsApp-based appointment booking service.

1. Information We Collect

  • Your WhatsApp phone number
  • Messages you send to the chatbot
  • Appointment details (name, date, time, reason for visit)
  • Timestamps of interactions

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Book and confirm appointments at your requested clinic
  • Send appointment reminders and confirmations
  • Improve the quality of our automated service

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share appointment information only with the clinic you are booking with.

4. Data Retention

Appointment data is retained for up to 12 months. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

5. Security

All data is transmitted over encrypted connections.
